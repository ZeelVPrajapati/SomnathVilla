SOMNATH VILL

3.5 & 4 Bedroom, Hall and Kitchen.

Presenting Somnath Villa, luxuriously designed and executed 3.5 & 4 BHK villas set in the lush green and opulent Aava County Campus, which is part of the larger Kensafari Golf Campus off Waghodia Road.

Villas with a perfect mix of classical & contemporary architecture, these homes exude a timeless charm.

Get the advantage of living in a well developed community with multiple leisure amenities, well planned infrastructure and abundant greenery. Enjoy the good life!

Welcome to a home where modern minimalism meets timeless sophistication. Discover unparalleled elegance in this stunning villa, a masterpiece of architectural brilliance. Every detail has been meticulously crafted, making this residence a harmonious blend of grandeur and contemporary luxury.

Stately columns grace the facade, adding an air of royalty. Double height living spaces redefine luxury, bathing the interiors in natural light.

Residences at Somnath Villa are not just a home, they are a celebration of a lifestyle where nature and sophistication dance in perfect harmony. In the heart of this exquisite campus, impeccable planning meets nature's abundance, crafting a classy ambiance that captivates the senses. Lush greenery intertwines with thoughtful design, creating a campus of serenity. Each element, from vibrant landscapes to elegant spaces,is carefully handpicked to ensure exclusivity.
